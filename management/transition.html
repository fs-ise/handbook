Pages that require an update for the transition

To mark pages that require changes, the transition_status and transition_comment fields can be added to the yaml header as follows:

---
title: "Resources"
transition_status: "toupdate"
transition_comment: "Update keys, lab access, printing/scanning, design, archive"
---

contents ....

Once the transition is implemented, the transition_status can be changed to updated.
